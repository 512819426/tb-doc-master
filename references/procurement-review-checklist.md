# Procurement Review Checklist

## Project facts

Extract:

- Project name and number
- Purchaser and procurement agency
- Procurement method
- Package/lot name and number
- Budget, maximum price, and price ceiling
- Funding source if stated
- Delivery or service location
- Delivery period, service period, warranty period
- Bid deadline, opening time, clarification deadline
- Submission platform, encryption, upload, sealing, and file naming rules

## Eligibility requirements

Check:

- Business license and legal existence
- Qualification certificates and validity
- Confidentiality, military, information security, system integration, software capability, quality, or other special certificates
- Financial status, tax payment, social insurance
- Credit record and blacklist rules
- Similar performance requirements
- Personnel qualifications
- Manufacturer authorization
- Consortium and subcontracting restrictions
- Relationship restrictions between bidders

## Bid document composition

Extract every required document and form. Mark whether it is:

- Mandatory
- Scored
- Supporting evidence
- Format-controlled
- Signature/seal-controlled
- Price-related

## Technical requirements

For each requirement, capture:

- Requirement text
- Mandatory or optional nature
- Whether negative deviation is allowed
- Evidence required
- Response chapter
- Test, acceptance, delivery, training, service, or warranty requirement

## Commercial and contract requirements

Check:

- Payment terms
- Tax and invoice requirements
- Delivery and acceptance
- Warranty and after-sales
- Liability for breach
- IP ownership
- Confidentiality obligations
- Data security and cyber security
- Personnel management
- Change control
- Termination clauses
- Dispute resolution

Do not make legal conclusions; identify issues for confirmation.

## Format and submission

Check:

- Required volume separation
- Original/copy count
- Electronic file type
- Page size, font, binding, cover, seal, and signature
- Authorized representative requirements
- Bid bond or guarantee format
- File upload naming and encryption
- Deadline and time zone

## Disqualification clauses

Extract every clause that may cause invalid bid, rejection, or non-responsive determination. Repeat this extraction in the risk list.
