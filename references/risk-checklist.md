# Risk Checklist

## Risk levels

- High risk: may cause invalid bid, disqualification, rejection, or failure to pass eligibility/compliance review.
- Medium risk: may cause major score loss, clarification burden, or unfavorable contract commitment.
- Low risk: may affect completeness, presentation, or review efficiency.

## Disqualification risks

Check:

- Missing mandatory document or form
- Wrong bidder name, project name, package number, or legal representative information
- Missing signature, seal, authorization, or date
- Qualification certificate missing, expired, not matching bidder, or not covering required scope
- Bid bond missing, late, wrong amount, wrong form, wrong beneficiary, or wrong validity
- Price exceeds ceiling or has inconsistent totals
- Multiple quotations where only one is allowed
- Consortium/subcontracting violation
- Bid validity shorter than required
- Failure to follow required format or electronic submission rules
- Technical or commercial terms marked as non-deviable are not satisfied
- Credit or blacklist restriction not addressed

## Commercial risks

Check:

- Payment terms beyond company tolerance
- Delivery period unrealistic
- Warranty or service commitment too broad
- Penalty, liability, or indemnity clauses
- IP ownership and source code delivery
- Data security and privacy commitments
- Acceptance standard unclear or strict
- Price adjustment, tax, invoice, and cost inclusion rules

Mark these for business/legal/finance confirmation when uncertain.

## Technical risks

Check:

- Mandatory parameter not covered
- Negative deviation hidden in narrative
- Required compatibility, localization, security, or performance not supported
- Acceptance test not addressed
- Required deliverables missing
- Integration boundary unclear
- Existing system migration risk omitted

## Defense/classified risks

Check:

- Confidentiality qualification requirement
- Classified personnel requirement
- Classified carrier handling
- Classified site or environment requirement
- Domestic substitution or controlled component requirement
- Security review, inspection, or acceptance requirement
- Prohibited outsourcing/subcontracting
- Confidentiality commitment beyond actual capability

## Final risk table

Use:

| Level | Risk | Source clause | Impact | Required action | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Owner may be left blank if the user has not assigned responsibilities.
